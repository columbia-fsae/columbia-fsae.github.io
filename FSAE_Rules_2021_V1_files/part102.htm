V.2.2 Visibility

a. The driver must have sufficient visibility to the front and sides of the vehicle

b. When seated in a normal driving position, the driver must have a minimum field of vision of 100Â° to both sides

c. If mirrors are required to meet this rule, they must remain in place and adjusted to enable the required visibility throughout all dynamic events.
