F.1.10 Primary Structure‌

The combination of the following components:

 Main Hoop

 Front Hoop

 Roll Hoop Braces and Supports

 Side Impact Structure

 Front Bulkhead

 Front Bulkhead Support

 Any Frame Members, guides, or supports that transfer load from the Driver Restraint System
