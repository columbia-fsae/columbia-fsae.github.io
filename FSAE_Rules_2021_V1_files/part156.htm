F.5.9 External Items

F.5.9.1 Any item which meets all three of:

 Located outside the Component Envelope F.1.15

 Located above 350 mm from the ground

 Could load the Primary Structure during a rollover

F.5.9.2 External Items must meet one of the two conditions:

a. Be attached at a Hoop to Brace node or a fully Triangulated structural node

b. When not attached at a node as described above, then:

 Additional Structural bracing meeting F.3.2.1.o must be added to prevent bending loads

 Additional calculations must be performed to show the member will not fail in bending, even if unbraced
