F.7.1 General Requirements

F.7.1.1 The Structural Equivalency Spreadsheet must demonstrate that the design is equivalent to a welded frame in terms of energy dissipation, yield and ultimate strengths in bending, buckling and tension.

F.7.1.2 Composite and metallic monocoques have the same requirements.
