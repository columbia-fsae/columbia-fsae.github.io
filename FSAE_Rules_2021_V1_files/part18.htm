GR.2.8 Forfeit for Non Appearance

GR.2.8.1 It is the responsibility of each team to be in the right place at the right time.

GR.2.8.2 If a team is not present and ready to compete at the scheduled time, they forfeit their attempt at that event.

GR.2.8.3 There are no makeups for missed appearances.
