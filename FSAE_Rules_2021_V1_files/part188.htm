F.9.1 Location

F.9.1.1 Any portion of the Fuel System that is less than 350 mm above the ground, and all parts of the Fuel Tank, must be inside the Primary Structure (F.1.10).

F.9.1.2 In side view, any portion of the Fuel System must not project below the lower surface of the chassis
