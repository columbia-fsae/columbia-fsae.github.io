F.10.1 General Requirements

F.10.1.1 Accumulator Container(s) must be built of mechanically robust material.

F.10.1.2 The Accumulator Container must be made from a Nonflammable Material (F.1.18)

F.10.1.3 All Accumulator Containers must be designed to withstand forces from deceleration in all directions

F.10.1.4 The Accumulator Containers and mounting systems are subject to approval during SES review and Technical Inspection.
