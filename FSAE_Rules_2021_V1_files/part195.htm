F.11.1 Location

F.11.1.1 All Accumulator Containers must lie inside the Primary Structure (F.1.10).

F.11.1.2 When used, Outboard Wheel Motors and their connections must meet EV.5.1.3

F.11.1.3 Tractive System (EV.1.3) components including cables and wiring other than those in F.11.1.2 above must be contained inside one or both of:

 The Rollover Protection Envelope F.1.13

 Structure meeting F.5.13 Component Protection
