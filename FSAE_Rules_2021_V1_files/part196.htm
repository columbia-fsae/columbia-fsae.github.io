F.11.2 Protection

F.11.2.1 All Accumulator Containers must be protected from side impact or rear impact by Side Impact Structure (F.6.4, F.7.6, or Equivalent)

 The Accumulator Container must not form part of the equivalent structure.

F.11.2.2 Motors must meet the protective requirements in Section EV.5.1

F.11.2.3 Tractive System components in a position below 350 mm from the ground must be:

a. Protected from:

 Side impact

 Rear impact

 Intrusion by non crushable objects (such as a differential)

b. Protected by structure meeting F.5.13 Component Protection
