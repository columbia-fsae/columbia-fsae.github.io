T.1.5 Driver’s Seat

T.1.5.1 The Driver’s Seat must be protected by one of the following:

a. In side view, the lowest point of any Driver’s Seat must be no lower than the upper surface of the lower frame rails

b. A longitudinal tube (or tubes) that meets the requirements for Side Impact tubing (F.3.2.1.e), passing underneath the lowest point of the Driver Seat.
