T.1.7 Floor Closeout

T.1.7.1 All vehicles must have a Floor Closeout to prevent track debris from entering

T.1.7.2 The Floor Closeout must extend from the foot area to the firewall

T.1.7.3 The panel(s) must be made of a solid, non brittle material.

T.1.7.4 If multiple panels are used, gaps between panels must not exceed 3 mm.
