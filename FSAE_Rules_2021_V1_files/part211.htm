T.2.3 Harness Requirements

T.2.3.1 Vehicles with a Reclined Driving Position must have:

a. A 6 Point Harness or a 7 Point Harness

b. Anti-Submarine Belts with tilt lock adjusters (“quick adjusters”) OR two sets of Anti- Submarine Belts installed.

T.2.3.2 All Lap Belts must incorporate a tilt lock adjuster (“quick adjuster”).

Lap Belts with “pull-up” adjusters are recommended over “pull-down” adjusters.

T.2.3.3 The Shoulder Belts must be the over the shoulder type. Only separate shoulder straps are permitted. “Y” type shoulder straps are not allowed. The “H” type configuration is allowed.
