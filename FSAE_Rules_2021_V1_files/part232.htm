T.5.6 Catch Cans

T.5.6.1 Separate catch cans must be employed to retain fluids from any vents for the engine coolant system and engine lubrication system.

Each catch can must have a minimum capacity of 10% of the fluid being contained or 0.9 liter, whichever is greater.

T.5.6.2 Any vent on other systems containing liquid lubricant or coolant, including a differential, gearbox, or electric motor, must have a catch can with a minimum capacity of 10% of the fluid being contained or 0.5 liter, whichever is greater.

T.5.6.3 Catch cans must be:

a. Capable of containing boiling water without deformation

b. Located rearwards of the Firewall below the driverâ€™s shoulder level

c. Positively retained, using no tie wraps or tape

T.5.6.4 Any catch can on the cooling system must vent through a hose with a minimum internal diameter of 3 mm down to the bottom levels of the Chassis.
