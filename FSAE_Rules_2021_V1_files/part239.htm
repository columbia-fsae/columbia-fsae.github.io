T.7.3 Measurement

T.7.3.1 All Aerodynamic Device limitations are measured:

a. With the wheels pointing in the straight ahead position

b. Without a driver in the vehicle

The intent is to standardize the measurement, see GR.4.4.1

T.7.3.2 Any part of any Aerodynamic Device or Bodywork must meet V.1.1

T.7.3.3 Head Restraint Plane

A transverse vertical plane through the rearmost portion of the front face of the driver head restraint support, excluding any padding, set (if adjustable) in its fully rearward position
