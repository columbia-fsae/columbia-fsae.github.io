T.7.5 Width

In plan view, any part of any Aerodynamic Device must be:

T.7.5.1 When forward of the centerline of the front wheel axles:

Inboard of two vertical planes parallel to the centerline of the chassis touching the outside of the front tires at the height of the hubs.

T.7.5.2 When between the centerlines of the front and rear wheel axles:

Inboard of a line drawn connecting the outer surfaces of the front and rear tires at the height of the wheel centers

T.7.5.3 When rearward of the Head Restraint Plane:

Inboard of two vertical planes parallel to the centerline of the chassis touching the inside of the rear tires at the height of the hub centerline.
