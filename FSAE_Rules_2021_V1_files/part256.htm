VE.1.5 Transponder

VE.1.5.1 Each vehicle must have a functional, properly mounted transponder of the specified type(s).

VE.1.5.2 Vehicles without a transponder may not be allowed to compete in any event for which a transponder is used.

VE.1.5.3 The approved transponder type(s) and mounting details will be provided on the FSAE Online Website or by the organizer
