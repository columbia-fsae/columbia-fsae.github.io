VE.3.1 General

VE.3.1.1 Any Driver Equipment:

a. Must be in good condition with no tears, rips, open seams, areas of significant wear, abrasions or stains which might compromise performance.

b. Must fit properly

c. May be inspected at any time VE.3.1.2 Flame Resistant Material

For this section some, but not all, of the approved materials are: Carbon X, Indura, Nomex, Polybenzimidazole (common name PBI) and Proban.

VE.3.1.4 Officials may impound any non approved Driver Equipment until the end of the competition.
