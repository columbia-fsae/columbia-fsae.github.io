IC.4.2 Commercial ETC System

IC.4.2.1 An ETC system that is commercially available, but does not comply with the regulations, may be used, if approved prior to the event.

IC.4.2.2 To obtain approval, submit a Rules Question which includes:

 Which ETC system the team is seeking approval to use.

 The specific ETC rule(s) that the commercial system deviates from.

 Sufficient technical details of these deviations to determine the acceptability of the commercial system.
