IC.5.5 Fuel Tank Filling

IC.5.5.1 During fueling or refueling the vehicle may only be touched by the fuel crew and officials. IC.5.5.2 The tank will be filled to the fill line, or if a filling system is used, to the automatic stop point.

IC.5.5.3 If, for any reason, the fuel level changes after the team have moved the vehicle, then no additional fuel will be added, unless fueling after Endurance, see D.13.2.5
