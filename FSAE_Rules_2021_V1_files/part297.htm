IC.5.6 Venting Systems

IC.5.6.1 Venting systems for the fuel tank and fuel delivery system must be designed such that fuel cannot spill during hard cornering or acceleration.

IC.5.6.2 All fuel vent lines must be equipped with a check valve to prevent fuel leakage when the tank is inverted.

IC.5.6.3 All fuel vent lines must exit outside the bodywork.
