EV.3.3 Lockout

EV.3.3.1 The TSMS EV.8.10.3 must be locked in the OFF position when any work is done on the vehicle.

EV.3.3.2 Until Electrical Inspection Completion IN.4.7, the HVD EV.6.5 must be disconnected when vehicles are:

a. Moved around the competition site

b. Participating in Static Events
