EV.3.4 Accumulator

EV.3.4.1 The following work activities at competition are allowed only in the designated area and during Electrical Technical Inspection IN.4 See EV.6.3.3

a. Opening Accumulator Containers

b. Any work on Accumulators, cells, or Segments

c. Energized electrical work

EV.3.4.2 Moving Accumulator cells and/or Accumulator Segment(s) at the competition site is only allowed inside a completely closed Accumulator Container. See EV.5.10.2
