EV.4.1.3 Regenerating energy is allowed and unrestricted when the vehicle speed is > 5 km/hr. EV.4.1.4 Supplying power to the motor such that the vehicle is driven in reverse is prohibited. EV.4.2 Energy Meter‌

EV.4.2.1 All Electric Vehicles must run with the Energy Meter provided by the organizer.

Refer to the FSAEOnline Website AD.2.2 for detail information on the Energy Meter.

EV.4.2.2 The Energy Meter must be installed in an easily accessible location

EV.4.2.3 All power supplying the Tractive System must flow through the Energy Meter. EV.4.2.4 Power and Voltage limits will be checked by the Energy Meter data.

Energy is calculated as the time integrated value of the measured voltage multiplied by the measured current logged by the Energy Meter.
