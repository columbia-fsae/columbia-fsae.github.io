EV.4.3 Violations‌

EV.4.3.1 A Violation occurs when one or both of the following exist:

a. Use of more than the specified maximum power EV.4.1.1

b. Exceed the maximum voltage EV.4.1.2

for one or both conditions:

 Continuously for 100 ms or more

 After a moving average over 500 ms is applied

EV.4.3.2 Missing Energy Meter data due to the team’s fault, tampering, or attempting to tamper with the Energy Meter will be treated as a Violation.

EV.4.3.3 Tampering, or attempting to tamper with the Energy Meter or its data may result in Disqualification (DQ)
