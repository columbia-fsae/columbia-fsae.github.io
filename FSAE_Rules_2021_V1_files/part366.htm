EV.8.2 Shutdown Circuit Operation

EV.8.2.1 The Shutdown Circuit must Open when any of the following exist:

a. Operation of, or detection from any of the components listed in EV.8.1.1

b. Any shutdown of the GLV System EV.8.2.2 When the Shutdown Circuit is Opened:

a. The Tractive System must be Shutdown

b. All Accumulator current flow must stop immediately

c. The voltage in the Tractive System must be Low Voltage EV.1.2 in five seconds or less

d. The Motor(s) must spin free. No torque must be applied to the Motor(s) EV.8.2.3 When the Shutdown Circuit is Opened by the AMS, IMD or BSPD:

a. The Tractive System must remain disabled until manually reset

b. The driver must not be able to reactivate the Tractive System from inside the vehicle

c. Operation of the Shutdown Buttons or TSMS must not reset the Shutdown Circuit

d. The Tractive System must be reset only by manual action of a person directly at the vehicle.
