EV.8.9 Interlocks‌

EV.8.9.1 Interlocks must be incorporated where designated (refer to EV.5.1.3, EV.6.5.2, EV.6.9 ) EV.8.9.2 Additional Interlocks may be included in the Tractive System or components

EV.8.9.3 The Interlock is a wire or connection that must:

a. Open the Shutdown Circuit EV.8.2.2 if the Interlock connection is broken or interrupted

b. Not be in the low (ground) connection to the AIR coils of the Shutdown Circuit EV.8.9.4 Interlock circuits or connections do not require physical separation from Tractive System

wiring or components
