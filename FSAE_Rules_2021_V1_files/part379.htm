EV.9.3 Ready to Drive

EV.9.3.1 The vehicle is Ready to Drive as soon as the Motor(s) will respond to the input of the APPS. EV.9.3.2 Closing the Shutdown Circuit alone must not set the vehicle to Ready to Drive mode.

EV.9.3.3 Additional actions must be performed by the driver to set the vehicle to Ready to Drive mode, such as pressing a dedicated start button after the Tractive System has been activated.

EV.9.3.4 Entry into Ready to Drive must be possible only while the brake pedal is pressed to engage the mechanical brakes T.3.1
