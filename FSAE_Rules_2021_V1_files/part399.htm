IN.2 INSPECTION PROCESS‌

Inspection will consist of the following parts:

 Initial Inspection

 Electrical Technical Inspection (EV only)

 Driver Cockpit Checks

 Mechanical Technical Inspection

 Tilt Test

 Rain Test (EV only)

 Noise and Master Switch Test (IC only)

 Brake Test
