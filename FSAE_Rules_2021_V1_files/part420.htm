IN.8.1 Inspection Items‌

The following items must be brought to Mechanical Technical Inspection:

 Vehicle on Dry Tires V.4.3.1

 Technical Inspection Form

 Push Bar VE.2.2

 Structural Equivalency Spreadsheet (SES) F.2.4

 Monocoque Laminate Test Specimens (if applicable) F.4.3

 The Impact Attenuator that was tested (if applicable) F.8.7

 Accumulator Container samples (EV only) (if applicable) F.10.2.2.h

 Tractive System Firewall sample (EV only) T.1.9.2
