IN.12.3 Brake Test Conduct (EV Only)

IN.12.3.1 Brake Test procedure:

a. Accelerate to speed until reaching the designated area

b. Switch off the Tractive System

c. Apply the brakes with force sufficient to demonstrate full lockup of all four wheels IN.12.3.2 The Brake Test is passed when all four wheels lock while the Tractive System is shut down IN.12.3.3 The Tractive System Active Light may switch shortly after the vehicle has come to a complete

stop as the reduction of the system voltage is not immediate. See EV.8.2.2.c
