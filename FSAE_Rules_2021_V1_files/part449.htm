IN.13.3 Inspection Validity

IN.13.3.1 Inspection Stickers may be removed from vehicles that are not in compliance with the Rules or are required to be Reinspected.

IN.13.3.2 Inspection Approval is valid only for the duration of the specific Formula SAE competition during which the inspection is conducted.
