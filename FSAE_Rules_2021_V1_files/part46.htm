GR.7.1 Student Developed Vehicle

GR.7.1.1 Vehicles entered into Formula SAE competitions must be conceived, designed, fabricated and maintained by the student team members without direct involvement from professional engineers, automotive engineers, racers, machinists or related professionals.

GR.7.1.2 Information Sources

The student team may use any literature or knowledge related to design and information from professionals or from academics as long as the information is given as a discussion of alternatives with their pros and cons.

GR.7.1.3 Professional Assistance

Professionals must not make design decisions or drawings. The Faculty Advisor may be required to sign a statement of compliance with this restriction.

GR.7.1.4 Student Fabrication

Students should perform all fabrication tasks
