S.3.16 Cost Event Scoring

S.3.16.1 Scoring Term Definitions

 Adjusted Cost – reported cost (incorporating Cost Addendum) plus adjustments and penalties

 Pyour - the Adjusted Cost of your vehicle

 Pmin - the Adjusted Cost of the lowest cost vehicle

 Pmax - the Adjusted Cost of the highest cost vehicle

S.3.16.2 Price Score is given by


Price Score =

40 x

( Pmax / Pyour ) -1



( Pmax / Pmin ) -1

S.3.16.3 Cost Event Score is the Sum of:

 Price Score 40 points maximum

 Discussion Score 40 points maximum

 Scenario Score 20 points maximum

 Penalty Points deducted from score, no limit for a Total Score of: 100 points maximum
