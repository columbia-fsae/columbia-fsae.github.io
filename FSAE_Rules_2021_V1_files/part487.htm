S.4.1 Design Event Objective

S.4.1.1 The Design Event evaluates the engineering effort that went into the vehicle and how the engineering meets the intent of the market both in terms of vehicle performance and overall value.

S.4.1.2 The team and vehicle that illustrate the best use of engineering to meet the design goals, a cost effective high performance vehicle, and the best understanding of the design by the team members will win the Design Event.
