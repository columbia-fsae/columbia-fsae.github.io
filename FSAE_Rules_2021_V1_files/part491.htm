S.4.5 Vehicle Condition

S.4.5.1 Inspection Approval IN.13.1.1 is not required prior to Design judging.

S.4.5.2 Vehicles must be presented for Design judging in finished condition, fully assembled, complete and ready to run.

S.4.5.3 The judges will not evaluate any vehicle that is presented at the Design event in what they consider to be an unfinished state.

S.4.5.4 Point penalties may be assessed for vehicles with obvious preparation issues
