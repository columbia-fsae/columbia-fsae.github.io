D.3.3 Driving Under Power

D.3.3.1 Vehicles must move under their own power only when inside the designated Dynamic Area(s), unless otherwise directed by the officials.

D.3.3.2 Driving a vehicle outside of scheduled events or scheduled practice will result in a 200 point penalty for the first violation and disqualification for a second violation.
