D.4.2 Informational Flags

D.4.2.1 An Information Flag communicates to the driver, but requires no specific action.

D.4.2.2 Red and Yellow Striped Flag - Something is on the racing surface that should not be there. Be prepared for evasive maneuvers to avoid the situation.

D.4.2.3 White Flag - There is a slow moving vehicle on the course. Be prepared to approach it at a cautious rate.
