D.5.1 Operating Adjustments

D.5.1.1 The organizer may alter the conduct and scoring of the competition based on weather conditions.

D.5.1.2 No adjustments will be made to times for running in differing Operating Conditions.

D.5.1.3 The minimum performance levels to score points may be adjusted by the Officials.
