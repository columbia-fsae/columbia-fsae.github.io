D.7.1 Three Event Limit

D.7.1.1 An individual team member may not drive in more than three events.

D.7.1.2 The Efficiency Event is considered a separate event although it is conducted simultaneously with the Endurance Event.

A minimum of four drivers are required to participate in all of the dynamic events.
