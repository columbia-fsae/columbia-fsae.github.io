AD.2.2 FSAE Online Website‌

The FSAE Online website is at: http://fsaeonline.com/

AD.2.2.1 Documents, forms, and information are accessed from the “Series Resources” link

AD.2.2.2 Each registered team must have an account on the FSAE Online Website.

AD.2.2.3 Each team must have one or more persons as Team Captain. The Team Captain must accept Team Members.

AD.2.2.4 Only persons designated Team Members or Team Captains are able to upload documents to the website.
