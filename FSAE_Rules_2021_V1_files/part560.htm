D.13.4 Efficiency Alternate Scoring (EV Only)

The organizer may decide to not measure Efficiency for Electric Vehicles that are run in their own class. When Efficiency is not measured:

a. The decision will be communicated prior to the start of the Endurance event

b. Each vehicle that is eligible for Efficiency points per D.13.3 will be awarded 100 points for Efficiency
