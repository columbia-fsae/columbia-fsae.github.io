AD.4.1 Preliminary Registration

AD.4.1.1 All students and faculty must be affiliated to your respective school /college/university on the Event Website by January 31 of the year of the competition.

AD.4.1.2 International student participants (or unaffiliated Faculty Advisors) who are not SAE International members must create a free customer account profile on www.sae.org. Upon completion, please email collegiatecompetitions@sae.org the assigned customer number stating also the event and university affiliation.
