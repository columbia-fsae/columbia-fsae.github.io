AD.5.2 Electrical System Officer (EV Only)‌

The Electrical System Officer (ESO) is responsible for all electrical operations of the vehicle during the event.

AD.5.2.1 Every participating team must appoint one or more ESO for the event AD.5.2.2 The ESO must meet the following:

a. Is a valid team member, see AD.3 Individual Participation Requirements

b. One or more ESO must not be a driver.

c. Is certified or has received appropriate practical training whether formal or informal for working with High Voltage systems in automotive vehicles.

Details of the training must be provided to the organizers on the ESO/ESA form for approval.
