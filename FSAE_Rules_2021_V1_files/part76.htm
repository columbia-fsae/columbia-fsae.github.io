AD.6.4 Waitlist

AD.6.4.1 Waitlisted teams must submit all documents by the same deadlines as registered teams to remain on the Waitlist.

AD.6.4.2 Once a team withdraws from the competition, the organizer will inform the next team on the Waitlist by email (the individual who registered the team to the Waitlist) that a spot on the registered list has opened.

AD.6.4.3 The team will then have 24 hours to accept or reject the position and an additional 24 hours to have the registration payment completed or in process.
