DR.3 SUBMISSION PENALTIES‌

DR.3.1.1 Each team is responsible for confirming that their documents have been properly uploaded or submitted and that the deadlines have been met.

DR.3.1.2 Prior to the Submission Deadline:

a. Documents may be uploaded at any time

b. Uploads may be replaced with new uploads without penalty

DR.3.1.3 If a Submitted Document revision is requested by the Reviewer, a new Submission Deadline for the revised document may apply

DR.3.1.4 Teams will not be notified if a document is submitted incorrectly.
