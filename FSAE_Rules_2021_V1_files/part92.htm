DR.3.3 Removal of Team Entry‌

DR.3.3.1 The organizer may remove the team entry when the:

a. Identified documents are Not Submitted in 10 days or less from the deadline. Removals will take place after each Document Submission deadline.

b. Team does not respond to Reviewer requests or organizer communications DR.3.3.2 When a team entry will be removed:

a. The team will be notified prior to cancelling registration.

b. No refund of entry fees will be given.
