DR.3.4 Specific Penalties

DR.3.4.1 Electronic Throttle Control (ETC)

a. There is no point penalty for ETC documents.

b. The team will not be allowed to run ETC on their vehicle and must use mechanical throttle operation when:

 The ETC Notice of Intent is Not Submitted

 The ETC FMEA is Not Submitted, or is not accepted DR.3.4.2 Fuel Type

There is no point penalty for a late fuel type order. Once the deadline has passed, the team will be allocated the basic fuel type.
